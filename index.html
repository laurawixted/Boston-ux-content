Shaping what's next

A deeper look at the ideas surfaced during our first gathering to start shaping what this community can become

View details

Details

Our second meetup was a big brainstorm and discussion about the purpose of the meetup. We used a Mad Lib exercise—"This group provides (value), (value), and (value) to (who) so they can (impact)"—to clarify our mission and vision.

We ran out of time to consolidate our ideas into a final statement, but examining the responses gave us valuable insights into the community's needs and aspirations and could be generalized as follows:

"The Boston UX Content meetup provides in-person content design workshops, knowledge sharing, and growth opportunities to the Boston area content designers and content adjacent designers so they can grow as content professionals and find community."

About us

The Boston UX Content meetup provides in-person content design discussions, workshops, knowledge sharing, and growth opportunities to the Boston area content designers and content adjacent designers so they can grow as content professionals and find community.

Who we are

We're a small but growing group of UX content professionals in the Boston area. We've been meeting since December 2025, connecting about the challenges and joys of our work with people who care about the role of language in digital product experiences.

We work across industries: edtech, fintech, healthcare, biotech, e-commerce, civic tech, and more. What unites us is a shared passion for clear, useful, human communication.
